# Privacy Policy - PureScan

**Last Updated: January 2026**

The **PureScan** app was built as a Commercial app. This service is provided by ANTONIO LUIZ DA COSTA NUNES and is intended for use as is.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
PureScan is designed to be a privacy-first application. 
- **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
- **Local Processing:** All document scanning and PDF generation are performed locally on your device using the Google ML Kit API. 
- **No Cloud Storage:** Your scanned documents are stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to your files.

### 2. Permissions
To function correctly, PureScan requires:
- **Camera:** To capture images of the documents you wish to scan.
- **Storage/Media:** To save the generated PDF files to your device.

### 3. Third-Party Services
This app uses Google Play Services (ML Kit). You can find their privacy policy here:
[Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 4. Security
We value your trust. Since all data stays on your device, the security of your documents depends on your device's security settings.
